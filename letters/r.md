## R

|     Word       |        Explanation            |Link (If needed)             |
|----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|
|Retargeting|`"Advertising strategy that shows ads to users who have previously visited your website, reminding them to return and complete a purchase."`|-|
|Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)|`"A marketing metric that measures revenue generated for every dollar spent on advertising, calculated as Revenue / Ad Spend."`|-|
|Return Policy|`"The rules and conditions under which customers can return products for refund or exchange, a key factor in purchase decisions."`|-|
|Revenue|`"The total income generated from sales before expenses are deducted, the top line of your financial statements."`|-|
|Reviews|`"Customer feedback and ratings displayed on product pages that influence purchase decisions and build trust with potential buyers."`|-|
|Responsive Design|`"Web design approach that ensures websites adapt and display properly across all device sizes, from desktop to mobile."`|-|
|REST API|`"Representational State Transfer - a standard architecture for building APIs that allow different ecommerce systems to communicate."`|-|
|Recurring Revenue|`"Predictable income from subscription products or services that customers pay for on a regular schedule."`|-|
|Referral Program|`"A marketing strategy that rewards existing customers for referring new customers to your store."`|-|
|Refund|`"Money returned to a customer after a product return, order cancellation, or service issue."`|-|
