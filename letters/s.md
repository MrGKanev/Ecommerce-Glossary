## S

|     Word       |        Explanation            |Link (If needed)             |
|----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|
|SEO (Search Engine Optimization)|`"Strategies to improve website visibility in organic search results, driving free traffic through better rankings."`|-|
|SKU (Stock Keeping Unit)|`"A unique identifier assigned to each product variant for inventory tracking and management purposes."`|-|
|Shopify|`"A leading SaaS ecommerce platform that enables merchants to create online stores without technical expertise."`|[Shopify](https://www.shopify.com)|
|Shopping Cart|`"Virtual container where customers collect products before checkout, essential for the purchase flow."`|-|
|Social Commerce|`"Selling products directly through social media platforms like Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook without leaving the app."`|-|
|Subscription Commerce|`"Business model where customers pay recurring fees for regular product deliveries or access to services."`|-|
|SSL Certificate|`"Security protocol that encrypts data between browsers and servers, indicated by HTTPS and essential for secure transactions."`|-|
|Shipping Rate|`"The cost charged to customers for delivering their orders, which can be flat rate, calculated, or free above certain thresholds."`|-|
|Split Testing|`"Another term for A/B testing - comparing two versions of a page or element to determine which performs better."`|-|
|Stripe|`"A popular payment processing platform for online businesses, known for developer-friendly APIs and global payment support."`|[Stripe](https://stripe.com)|
|Stock|`"The quantity of products available for sale, managed through inventory systems to prevent overselling."`|-|
|SaaS (Software as a Service)|`"Cloud-based software delivered via subscription, like Shopify or BigCommerce, without self-hosting requirements."`|-|
