## M

|     Word       |        Explanation            |Link (If needed)             |
|----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|
|Marketplace|`"An ecommerce platform where multiple third-party sellers can list and sell products, such as Amazon, eBay, or Etsy."`|-|
|Merchant of Record (MoR)|`"The entity legally responsible for processing customer payments and handling tax compliance, chargebacks, and refunds."`|-|
|Meta Tags|`"HTML elements that provide metadata about a webpage to search engines, including title tags, descriptions, and keywords for SEO."`|-|
|Microservices|`"An architectural approach where ecommerce applications are built as a collection of small, independent services that communicate via APIs."`|-|
|Mobile Commerce (M-Commerce)|`"Buying and selling goods through mobile devices, now accounting for over 70% of ecommerce traffic globally."`|-|
|Multi-Channel Selling|`"Selling products across multiple platforms and channels, including your own website, marketplaces, social media, and physical stores."`|-|
|Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)|`"The smallest number of units a supplier will sell at once, important for inventory planning and wholesale purchasing."`|-|
|Machine Learning|`"AI technology that enables personalized recommendations, dynamic pricing, fraud detection, and demand forecasting in ecommerce."`|-|
|Markdown|`"A text formatting syntax used for product descriptions, blog posts, and documentation that converts to HTML for web display."`|-|
