## N

|     Word       |        Explanation            |Link (If needed)             |
|----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|
|Net Promoter Score (NPS)|`"A customer loyalty metric that measures how likely customers are to recommend your brand to others, scored from -100 to +100."`|-|
|Niche Market|`"A specialized segment of the market with specific needs and preferences, often less competitive and more profitable for focused ecommerce stores."`|-|
|Native Checkout|`"A checkout experience that happens directly within a platform (like Instagram or TikTok) without redirecting to an external website."`|-|
|Next-Day Delivery|`"Shipping option that guarantees delivery by the next business day, now expected as standard by many online shoppers."`|-|
|No-Code Platform|`"Tools that allow building ecommerce stores and automations without any programming knowledge through visual drag-and-drop interfaces."`|-|
|Notification|`"Automated messages sent to customers via email, SMS, or push about order status, promotions, abandoned carts, or back-in-stock alerts."`|-|
|NFT Commerce|`"The sale of Non-Fungible Tokens representing digital goods, collectibles, or access passes through ecommerce platforms."`|-|
