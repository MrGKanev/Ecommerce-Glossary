## P

|     Word       |        Explanation            |Link (If needed)             |
|----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|
|Payment Gateway|`"A service that authorizes and processes credit card and digital payments between customers and merchants securely."`|-|
|PCI Compliance|`"Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard - requirements for securely handling credit card information to prevent fraud."`|[PCI DSS](https://www.pcisecuritystandards.org)|
|Personalization|`"Tailoring the shopping experience to individual customers based on their behavior, preferences, and purchase history using AI and data."`|-|
|Product Information Management (PIM)|`"Centralized software for managing all product data, descriptions, images, and attributes across multiple sales channels."`|-|
|Print on Demand (POD)|`"A fulfillment model where products are only printed/created after a customer orders, eliminating inventory risk for custom items."`|-|
|Progressive Web App (PWA)|`"Web applications that provide app-like experiences on mobile devices without requiring download from app stores."`|-|
|Push Notification|`"Clickable pop-up messages sent to users' devices for marketing, order updates, or re-engagement campaigns."`|-|
|Product Feed|`"A file containing structured product data submitted to shopping platforms like Google Shopping, Facebook, or comparison sites."`|-|
|Pricing Strategy|`"The methodology for setting product prices, including dynamic pricing, psychological pricing, bundle pricing, or value-based pricing."`|-|
|Pre-Order|`"Allowing customers to purchase products before they are available, helping gauge demand and secure early sales."`|-|
