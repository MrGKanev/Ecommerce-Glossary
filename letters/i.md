## I

|     Word       |        Explanation            |Link (If needed)             |
|----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|
|Inventory|`"The quantity and types of products a retailer has available for sale."`|-|
|Inbound Link|`"Links from external websites pointing to your site, valuable for SEO (also called backlinks)."`|-|
|Inbound Marketing|`"Attracting customers through valuable content, SEO, and social media rather than paid ads."`|-|
|Influencer Marketing|`"Partnering with social media personalities to promote products to their followers."`|-|
|Instagram Shopping|`"Feature allowing businesses to tag and sell products directly within Instagram posts and stories."`|-|
|Integration|`"Connecting different software systems to share data and automate workflows."`|-|
|Infinite Scroll|`"Loading more products as users scroll down, replacing traditional pagination."`|-|
|Invoice|`"Document detailing products purchased, prices, taxes, and payment terms."`|-|
