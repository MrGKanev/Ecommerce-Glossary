## C

|     Word       |        Explanation            |Link (If needed)             |
|----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|
|Call-To-Action (CTA)|`"Compelling text or buttons prompting visitors to take action, like 'Buy Now' or 'Add to Cart'."`|-|
|Cart Abandonment|`"When shoppers add items to cart but leave without purchasing, typically 70% of carts are abandoned."`|-|
|Conversion Rate|`"Percentage of visitors who complete a desired action, such as making a purchase."`|-|
|Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)|`"Systematic process of improving website elements to increase the percentage of visitors who convert."`|-|
|Content Management System (CMS)|`"Software for creating and managing digital content without coding knowledge."`|-|
|Cache|`"Stored data that speeds up page loading by reducing server requests."`|-|
|Chargeback|`"Forced transaction reversal initiated by the cardholder's bank, often due to disputes or fraud."`|-|
|Churn Rate|`"Percentage of customers who stop buying or cancel subscriptions within a given period."`|-|
|CSV (Comma Separated Values)|`"File format for importing/exporting product data, orders, and customer lists."`|-|
|Cookies|`"Small data files stored in browsers to remember user preferences, cart contents, and tracking data."`|-|
|Composable Commerce|`"Modern architecture using best-of-breed, API-first components instead of monolithic platforms."`|-|
|Customer Data Platform (CDP)|`"Software unifying customer data from all touchpoints into a single customer profile."`|-|
|Click-Through Rate (CTR)|`"Percentage of people who click on a link or ad after seeing it."`|-|
