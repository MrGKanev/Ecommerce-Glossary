## L

|     Word       |        Explanation            |Link (If needed)             |
|----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|
|Landing Page|`"A standalone web page designed specifically for marketing campaigns, optimized to convert visitors into leads or customers with a focused call-to-action."`|-|
|Last Mile Delivery|`"The final step of delivery from a distribution center to the customer's doorstep, often the most expensive and complex part of the fulfillment process."`|-|
|Lifetime Value (LTV)|`"The predicted total revenue a business can expect from a single customer account throughout their entire relationship with the brand."`|-|
|Live Shopping|`"Real-time video streaming where hosts demonstrate products and viewers can purchase instantly, combining entertainment with ecommerce."`|-|
|Loyalty Program|`"A rewards system that incentivizes repeat purchases by offering points, discounts, or exclusive benefits to returning customers."`|-|
|Logistics|`"The overall process of managing how products are acquired, stored, and transported to customers, including warehousing, inventory, and shipping."`|-|
|Low-Code Platform|`"Development platforms that enable building ecommerce applications with minimal coding, allowing faster deployment and easier customization."`|-|
|LLM (Large Language Model)|`"AI models like GPT that power chatbots, product descriptions, personalized recommendations, and customer service automation in modern ecommerce."`|-|
