## O

|     Word       |        Explanation            |Link (If needed)             |
|----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|
|Omnichannel|`"A seamless shopping experience across all channels (online, mobile, in-store) where customer data and inventory are unified."`|-|
|Order Management System (OMS)|`"Software that tracks and manages orders from multiple sales channels, handling fulfillment, inventory, and customer communication."`|-|
|Order Fulfillment|`"The complete process from receiving an order to delivering it to the customer, including picking, packing, and shipping."`|-|
|One-Click Checkout|`"A streamlined checkout process where returning customers can complete purchases with a single click using saved payment and shipping info."`|-|
|Open Source|`"Software with publicly available source code that can be modified and customized, popular for flexible ecommerce solutions like WooCommerce or Magento."`|-|
|Organic Traffic|`"Visitors who find your website through unpaid search results, as opposed to paid advertising."`|-|
|Out of Stock|`"When a product has zero available inventory, requiring either a backorder option, waitlist signup, or temporary listing removal."`|-|
|Outsourcing|`"Delegating business functions like fulfillment, customer service, or development to third-party providers to reduce costs or access expertise."`|-|
