## F

|     Word       |        Explanation            |Link (If needed)             |
|----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|
|Fulfillment|`"The process of receiving, processing, packing, and shipping customer orders."`|-|
|Fulfillment Center|`"Warehouse facilities that store inventory and process orders for ecommerce businesses."`|-|
|Free Shipping|`"Delivery without cost to the customer, often used as a conversion incentive above a minimum order value."`|-|
|Fraud Detection|`"Systems and algorithms that identify suspicious transactions to prevent chargebacks and losses."`|-|
|Funnel|`"The customer journey stages from awareness to purchase, visualizing where visitors drop off."`|-|
|First-Party Data|`"Customer data collected directly by your business, increasingly valuable as third-party cookies phase out."`|-|
|Flash Sale|`"Limited-time promotions creating urgency and driving quick purchase decisions."`|-|
|Faceted Search|`"Filter-based navigation allowing customers to narrow products by attributes like price, size, or color."`|-|
