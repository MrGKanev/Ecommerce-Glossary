## V

|     Word       |        Explanation            |Link (If needed)             |
|----------------|-------------------------------|-----------------------------|
|Value Proposition|`"A clear statement of the unique benefits and value your products or store offers compared to competitors."`|-|
|Variant|`"Different versions of the same product, such as different sizes, colors, or materials, each with its own SKU."`|-|
|Virtual Try-On|`"AR technology allowing customers to visualize products on themselves or in their space before purchasing."`|-|
|Voice Commerce|`"Shopping through voice-activated assistants like Alexa or Google Assistant, an emerging ecommerce channel."`|-|
|Vendor|`"A supplier or manufacturer that provides products for resale in your ecommerce store."`|-|
|VIP Program|`"Exclusive loyalty tier offering premium benefits to top customers based on spending or engagement levels."`|-|
|Viral Marketing|`"Marketing strategy designed to encourage rapid sharing, exponentially increasing brand awareness at low cost."`|-|
|Virtual Shopping Assistant|`"AI-powered chatbots that help customers find products, answer questions, and guide them through the purchase process."`|-|
